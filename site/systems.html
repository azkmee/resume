Systems
analyses.

I take systems apart to understand them. Products I admire, primitives I want to learn, architectures I want to argue with. Each teardown asks three questions: what is it? why does it feel right? what would I change?

Method

how I write these

01Observe

Use the thing for a week. Note micro-frictions, surprises, moments of delight. No reading source yet.

02Decompose

Sketch the system: surfaces, state, data flow, network calls, the latency budget. Look for the spine.

03Critique

Pick the trade-offs apart. What did they choose to be good at? What would I do differently — and why?
