About me

long form

By trade I'm a generalist engineer — I work across the stack and prefer it that way. Backend feels like architecture; frontend feels like craft; data plumbing feels like puzzles. I'm happiest when I can move between all three.

Off the clock I read about systems: how teams build them, how products earn their feel, how schemas evolve when nobody's watching. The /systems section is where I write that down.

I'm always open to a coffee about engineering culture, ESG tech, or careers in Singapore.
